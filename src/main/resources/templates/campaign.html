About the Campaign

We are delighted to introduce the "Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi" campaign, a noble initiative aimed at transforming the lives of orphanage students by providing them with the invaluable gift of coding education under our project ‘Coding on Wheels’.

Recognizing the power of education in fostering self-independence and a dignified life, we are reaching out to compassionate individuals, corporates, and well-to-do families to join hands in supporting this meaningful cause.

All donations are eligible for 50% tax exemption benefits under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act as we are working for a social cause.

How You Can Contribute

  • Financial Support: Your generous contributions will directly fund coding education programs for orphanage students. Every donation, regardless of size, plays a crucial role.
  • Spread the Word: Share this campaign across your networks and social media platforms to help us reach more supporters.
  • Corporate Sponsorship: We invite corporates to become partners. Corporate sponsorships can create a lasting impact on orphanage students' lives.

Your support can be the key to unlocking a world of possibilities for orphanage students.
Together, let's pave the way for a future where every child has the opportunity to build a dignified and independent life through education.

Corporate Training for Social Impact

"Shaping Futures: Join Us to Empower Orphan and Government School Students!"

Welcome to "Padho Bhi Padhon Bhi", a trailblazing campaign that seamlessly integrates corporate growth with social responsibility. In this unique initiative, we present an opportunity for companies and employees to engage in transformative technology training while contributing to a noble cause.

How It Works

Engage your employees in cutting-edge technology training, and in return, we request a minimum donation of 5000 rupees per participant. The accumulated donations will be directed towards providing coding education to orphanage and underprivileged students in government schools.

Note: Donations are eligible for 50% tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.

Why Engage

  • Dual Impact: Professional development aligned with philanthropy—each training session contributes to personal growth and community support.
  • Social Empowerment: Empower marginalized communities by contributing to coding education for orphanage and government school students.
  • Amplified CSR: Strengthen your CSR initiatives with a tangible, meaningful contribution.
  • Collective Progress: Advance your team’s skills while supporting the educational aspirations of underprivileged youth.

Ignite Change with Every Training Session: Revolutionize corporate training by making every learning opportunity a catalyst for social change.

Join us to embody the essence of shared success and social responsibility.
